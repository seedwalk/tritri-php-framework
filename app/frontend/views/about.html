About us

Focused at providing useful services, and pursuing the ideal to create a purposeful company, we founded BO Partners Inc in late 2013.

We imprinted the values we expect from a company that would provide services and defined the kind of culture we wanted to see in action.

We have worked extensively at developing the basis for our startup. We are proud of our values and the way we conduct business.

We believe in respect for others and we try to show this as a company in every interaction, in every task, with everyone both in and out of our company.

Our values

FOCUSED, USEFUL & RELIABLE
We concentrate on what we do better. Oustanding service, simple and helpful interactions, reasonable pricing and respect for your time, money and trust.

PASSIONATE & COLLABORATIVE
Our crew work help you to conquer your challenges. We believe that passion, as well as a positive and optimistic attitude, inspire others.

ENTHUSIAST & COURAGEOUS
We love to help, even if it means questioning current structures. We embrace change, continuous learning is the way to deliver an oustanding service.

ETHICAL & RESPECTFUL
We provide our service with integrity, building our relationships on trust and mutual respect. We treat you the same way we want to be treated.
